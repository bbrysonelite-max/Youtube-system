5 agents every distributor needs running before 2026 ends.
Not theory. Not demos. The exact five I built when I realized AI was coming for me — and decided to build the factory instead.

I spent 30 years in person-to-person distribution.
When AI showed up, I had a choice: get replaced or get ahead.
These five agents are how I got ahead. Every one of them runs while I sleep.

The 5 Agents
01
The Follow-Up Agent
Watches your prospect list, surfaces who hasn't heard from you in 48 hours, and drafts the next message in your voice — ready to send in one click.
Replaces: the mental load of remembering who you owe a follow-up
02
The Research Agent
Before any prospect conversation, this agent pulls what matters — job, company, recent moves, potential pain points — and gives you a one-paragraph brief.
Replaces: 30 minutes of LinkedIn stalking before every call
03
The Objection Handler
Feed it the objection you just got. It drafts three response options in your voice — direct, empathetic, or disarming. You pick one, edit if you want, send.
Replaces: staring at a message not knowing what to say next
04
The Content Agent
Takes one idea you had today and turns it into a Short script, a LinkedIn post, and a story caption — formatted and ready for any platform.
Replaces: the 90 minutes you don't have to spend on content every day
05
The Morning Brief Agent
Every morning before you open anything else: your top 3 follow-ups, one piece of market intel, and your one focus for the day. Lands in your inbox at 7am.
Replaces: the scattered first hour that decides how the whole day goes
