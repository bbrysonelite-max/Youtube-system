## Voice Formatting Rules (for ElevenLabs rendering)
- Ellipses for natural pause: "Here's what nobody tells you..."
- Em dashes for emphasis rhythm: "This changes everything — and I mean everything."
- Short sentences after long ones. Always.
- Question marks mid-script: "You see what just happened there?"
- Never more than 2 commas in one sentence before a period.
- Flesch-Kincaid target: Grade 8 or below.
- Flag any sentence over 20 words for revision.
