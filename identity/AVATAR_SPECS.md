## Scene Segmentation Rule
HeyGen avatar quality degrades on very long scripts.
Break any script over 8 minutes into segments of 2-3 minutes each.
Stitch in post-production. Prevents "uncanny valley drift."

---

## Script Input Formatting for HeyGen
- Use [pause] tags for deliberate beats
- Ellipses (...) signal natural pause
- Em dashes (—) signal emphasis rhythm
- Keep sentences under 20 words for clean lip sync
- Always test first 60 seconds before rendering the full script
